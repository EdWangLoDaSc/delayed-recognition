An Atlas of Sleeping Beauties Kaboski · Amar Wang · Yadav

Premise · Some papers are ignored for years, then suddenly rediscovered. This atlas charts citation trajectories across fifty thousand works in computer science, physics, and molecular biology — ranked by Van Raan's Beauty Coefficient, a measure of how sharply a paper's reception awakens from dormancy.
