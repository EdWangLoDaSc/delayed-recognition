Data contract and cautions

Methods

The analytical object is an OpenAlex work with a DOI, a primary field assignment, yearly citation bins, and a local delay score. The corpus is a high-citation, stratified sample for interaction and hypothesis formation, not a neutral census of all papers in each discipline.
